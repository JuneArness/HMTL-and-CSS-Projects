Lundi à 21 h dès le 1er avril

About

The series follows the 20 or so members of the Luxurious Montréal Automobile Club, part of the “low rider” culture. We’ll try to understand what attracts these aficionados to such eccentric and flamboyant vehicles, as we tag along to car shows, night rides and endless evenings spent repairing and pimping these vintage rides.
